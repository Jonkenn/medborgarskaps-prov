## Why December 2026 Is Special for Swedish Holidays

Sweden’s public holidays fall on fixed dates rather than weekdays, which occasionally creates long uninterrupted breaks when they land near weekends. In 2026, Christmas, New Year’s, and Epiphany align almost perfectly.

### Key Swedish Bank Holidays in December 2026

- **December 24 — Christmas Eve** (treated as a full day off in most workplaces)  
- **December 25 — Christmas Day (public holiday)**  
- **December 26 — Boxing Day / Annandag jul (public holiday)**  
- **January 1 — New Year’s Day (public holiday)**  
- **January 6 — Epiphany / Trettondedag jul (public holiday)**  

With weekends factored in, these red days create natural “bridges” that can be covered with vacation — dramatically multiplying time off.
